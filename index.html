ABOUT FOOD FOR ALL

Food waste is a global problem, while millions of people struggle with hunger. Food For All aims to bridge this gap by connecting individuals and organizations with surplus food to those in need.

How it Works ?

  • Businesses and individuals list their surplus food.

  • Matching System

    Our platform connects donors with verified recipients.

  • Pick-up & Delivery

    Volunteers or recipients arrange pick-up or delivery.
